About

Your big day, made for the feed.

Hi, I'm Yas — the creator behind The Content Edit. I help newly-wed couples share their wedding moments in a way that feels authentic, cinematic, and completely scroll-stopping.

I specialise in short-form wedding videos crafted for TikTok and Instagram — think trending sounds, dreamy transitions, and the moments that actually made you laugh and cry. I also shoot with a digital camera to give you those gorgeous, candid, film-like stills that have their own story to tell.

Whether it's the first look, the reception, or the chaotic drive away — I'm there to catch it all, edit it with care, and hand it back to you ready to post.
